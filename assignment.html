principles of management

Principle of Clarity in Ideas: It's essential for the sender to have a clear understanding of what they want to communicate. When thoughts are clear, communication becomes more effective. Terry emphasizes the importance of fully informing oneself before communicating.

Principle of Attention: The receiver must understand the message clearly for communication to be successful. Simply transferring information is not enough; the receiver needs to be attentive and interested in the message.
